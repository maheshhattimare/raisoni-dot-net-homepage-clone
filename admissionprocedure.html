Addmission Guidelines

  • The pre requirement for the admission procedure for our undergraduate program involves completion of Class 12th education from a recognized Board.
  • The pre requirement for the admission procedure for our Postgraduate program involves completion of Graduate degree from a recognized University.
  • The applicants who have appeared in the Final year of their Graduation in the year 2018 are also eligible to apply for the Postgraduate programs.
  • There are age limit criteria for admission to the undergraduate program in the institute.

Admission Process

The applicant needs to check with the respective institutions with regards to the eligibility criteria. After which, they are required to follow the given stepwise process:

Step 1
Register Yourself
The applicant needs to register on RGI Admission Portal with their personal details and course they desire to seek admission.
Step 2
Verify the Mobile
The Applicant needs to verify mobile number through OTP and email id for correspondence
Step 3
Fill Application form online
The Applicant need to fill the application form online for the desired institute and course with preferences of specializations/ branches
Step 4
Pay the Application Fee
The Applicant need to pay the application fee towards the purchase of prospectus
Step 5
Submit the application
The Applicant needs to upload a few relevant documents and submit the application for provisional admission. (Institute has rights to verify the details you have entered in the application for the further processing of an application)
