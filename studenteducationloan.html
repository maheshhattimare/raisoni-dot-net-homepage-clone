Documents Required for Loan Processing

  • KYC for Parent (APPLICANT) (either Father or Mother - Mandatorily and income earning member) & KYC of C0-Applicant (Student cum Beneficiary)
    • Photo identity proof: Passport, PAN card, voters identity card, driving license, Aadhar Card (any one - Date of Birth details Mandatory)
    • Address proof: Ration card, passport, driving license, bank a/c statement, electricity / telephone bill, Aadhaar card. Any kind of bill submitted as a proof should not be older than 3 months (Any One). Gas bill mandatory.
    • Signature Proof: Passport, PAN card, Driving license - Any one
  • Recent color Photo of Loan Applicant and Co-applicant
  • Student School/College ID card (except in case of new admissions)
  • Admission confirmation from College (in case of new admission)
  • School / College fees Demand Proof
  • Loan Application form
  • Income document of Parent
