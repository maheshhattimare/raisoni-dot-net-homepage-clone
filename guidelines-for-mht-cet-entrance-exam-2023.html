Guidelines for MHT CET Entrance Exam 2023

The Maharashtra Health and Technical Common Entrance Test for the year 2023 have released their online application form on MHT CET 2023 Official Website (insert link). MHT-CET-2023 is the Entrance Examination for acceptance to professional courses like M.B.A., Engineering and Technology, Agriculture, Pharmacy, etc. As per the schedule for the online application and submission of the MHT CET 2023 application form by candidates in online mode is as follows. The final date to fill out the online form for MHT CET 2023 is March 31, 2023. The candidates, however, can still register till April 7, 2023, by bearing extra charges for late fee submission. The eligible applicants are required to fill up and submit the MHT CET online application form by registering their personal and academic details. The application procedure consists of MHT CET 2023 registration, filling up and submission of online application form, submitting essential documents, and fee payment.

Below given is a complete guide with step-by-step instructions to fill out MHT CET Online Application Form for the year 2023.

  • The eligible candidates are required to visit the official website for MHT CET registration.
  • To start with the registration process, click on the direct link, which will take you to the online registration form or on the homepage, click new registration.
  • It is necessary for the applicants to enter personal and basic information about themselves so as to complete the registration procedure for MHT CET.
  • As soon as the applicants submit their online registration form, they will be provided with their individual login credentials, which will be used by them to sign in and use their specific accounts allocated to them on the portal.
  • The applicants then will have to submit their academic details, communication details, etc., in their application form.
  • Once the applicant has finished filling out the form, then the individual needs to upload the necessary documents that are requested.
  • These documents have to be scanned and submitted along with the online form in a specific format.
  • After completion of filling out the registration form and uploading the necessary documents, the applicant is required to make the fee payment.
  • The applicant is always advised to keep a copy of the form and the payment receipt just in case if there comes to be a technical error.

Documents Required to Fill Out the MHT CET Application Form 2023

The applicants can go through the entire list of all the required documents in order to fill out the application form. The eligible applicants are advised to keep all the documents ready at the time of filling out the MHT CET 2023 application form.


Below given is the list of necessary documents for the MHT CET examination 2023
  • Below given is the list of necessary documents for the MHT CET examination 2023
  • D.O.B. proof.
  • Domicile certificate.
  • Caste certificate (if applicable).
  • Address proof.
  • Scanned images of a passport-sized photograph and signature.
  • Internet banking details or Credit/Debit card details for the payment of the fee.

The online application registration schedule and information brochure for this examination have been made available on the official website www.mahacet.org of the State Common Entrance Test Cell. This information is for all the concerned students/parents/ institutions/ State Holders.
