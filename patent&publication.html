The GHR Labs and Research Centre completely abide to the above and even more encompassing the research publications. Education breathes empowerment. there is held industry visits for the students to ingest in them the real procedure and the on-going developments in the realm.
OUR PROMISE
The most promising aspect for the students pursuing research in our laboratories is the assistance we provide in the form of patents and publications.

It has been the diligence, the procedure and the steps associated with our team, our students and our institute, we continue to inculcate in every endeavor, that in accordance with the Indian Ranking of applicants for patents for scientific and research and development organization, we have reached from 9th position in 2012-2013 to 3rd position in 2016-2017.


The year 2012- 2013 witnessed the G H Raisoni Labs and Research Centre applying for 10 patents. This enhances to a 31 number in the year 2014-15. The number continued to increase, to 33 for the year 2015-16 along with the patents from another esteemed institute of our umbrella, the G H Raisoni College of Engineering. And, then the year 2016-2017 we pursued a total of 50 applications for patent.


This is just a start of the journey guided by scientific intelligence and embedded with search for more data and facts. A total of 208 patents till date and counting!
