WHAT ARE THE BEST INNOVATION AWARDS?

Our institute has always believed in promoting research and innovation amongst the undergraduate, postgraduate students and those pursuing higher studies. In order to felicitate the excellence shown by the students in their projects, we give the Best Innovation Awards. It is a National level contest for students in a variety of disciplines, involving academicians, innovators, researchers and those studying in college. Complete information pertaining to the same can be availed at the institute or the website with ease.

The contest is held in 3 levels. The inclusive areas for nomination for the award include agriculture, sanitation, ICT, water, environment, healthcare and medicine, road safety, food processing, infrastructure, power & energy, transportation to name a few. As the name indicates the candidate is selected on the basis of the project which is innovative and at the same time has sprawling benefits at low cost.
