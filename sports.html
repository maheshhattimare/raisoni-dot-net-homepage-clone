Sports is a metaphor for overcoming obstacles and achieving against great odds. Athletes, in times of difficulty, can be important role models.- Bill Bradley


This is exactly in our mind when we encourage sports in all our colleges under the RGI umbrella. Development of uniqueness in character, the ‘never say never’ attitude and a raring attribute att-height-380 to fight till the last sweat drop are gifts of sports. And, we definitely aspire to endow it on our students. Education is not just about academic knowledge and its practical application. It is building of the personality, enriching human life and ingesting the finest character traits. Undoubtedly, all of this is possible with sports.


Thus, our curriculum has been so designed which inculcates rich academics along with sports activities in the day to day life of the students.


We envision our students to embrace the core values,


  • Respect
  • Appreciation
  • Involvement
  • Support
  • Objectivity
  • Neutrality
  • Innovation








We aim to develop the following attribute att-height-380s associated with sports in rgi


  • Development of sports forum
  • A sports coordinator assigned to every institut
  • Intra group sports competition to be organized periodically
  • Invite sports personality in the college to give lectures and take sessions to benefit students
  • Organization of a grand event in the college at the college or the University level
  • Awards for the sports personality of the year
  • Scholarships for the eligible sportsperson








Outdoor games

A plethora of outdoor games make sure to provide the much-needed fitness regime for students. Hence, we have the separate outdoor grounds for volleyball, cricket and football. Needless to add that we have made sure to include them with the latest equipment and apt field measures to suffice the students. Students can enjoy the sports of their choice or make sure to excel in their field in accordance with their capability and passion.

Indoor Games

We comprehend the need for indoor sports facilities for our students. Hence, we have developed table tennis, carom, chess, pool and other similar facilities in our indoor sports room. They are well equipped, well lit and well ventilated. There are sports teacher available to assist the students with any query or guide them better.

Sports In Charge

Every sport has a sport in charge in the respective colleges. These are proficient in their games. They are well aware of the rules and norms of the games to provide the requisite guidance to the students.

Competitions

Periodically inter- group competitions are organized in the college to motivate the sports person. Then, there is also the intercollegiate held to choose the better among the best.

Scholarships

The deserving candidates/students are always applauded by the RGI. Hence, we provide them with scholarships to do better in their field..
