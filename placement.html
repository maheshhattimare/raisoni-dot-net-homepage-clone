About Placement

"Raisoni Institutes consistently attracts the finest faculty and the best of students for its Bachelors and Master Program. The Institutes have a rich tradition of pursuing excellence and has continually re-invented itself in terms of academic programs, research and infrastructure. Students are exposed to challenging research based academics and host of sports, cultural and organizational activities on its vibrant campuses. The presence of the world class facilities, institute-industry collaborations, interdisciplinary research facilities and industrial training opportunities help students to excel and be prepared for the competitive world. In the last twenty-two years, Raisoni Institutions has produced many well-known alumni, whose contributions to the corporate & society have been significant. We highly value our partnership with recruiters, alumni and friends of Raisoni Institutes and are committed to making your recruiting experience productive and positive. I invite the recruiting organizations and graduating students to find the best match between their needs and capabilities.

Students at Raisoni Institutes continuously undergo Soft Skill and Cognitive skill training apart from their academics. Regular sessions of aptitude development are being held at each campus. Students in each semester undergo an assessment to test if they have gained knowledge and capabilities to match the ever growing expectation of the corporate world. At Raisoni Institutes we keep organizing various training programs and sessions by industry experts on latest technologies and industry expectations. This help enhancing the knowledge but also helps in managing the expectation of the students and their approach towards their life.

Recruitment Process

STEP

1

Online Students Registration

STEP

2

Invitation to Companies for Campus Placement Alumni Involvement Industry Connect through Industry Institute Interaction Cell

STEP

3

Companies willing to conduct Campus; connects us along with all relevant details like Job Profile, Job Description, Job Location, Package, etc.

STEP

4

Campus Placement Details are shared with students and students are asked to nominate themselves for teh said company and profile.

STEP

5

The Results of the campus recruitment drive is shared with the College.

STEP

6

Company Conducts the screening process which usually includes Aptitude Test, Group Discussion, Personal Interview

STEP

7

The Results of the campus recruitment drive is shared with the College.

STEP

8

At a future date - the joining dates are informed by company to the college & the shortlisted students.
