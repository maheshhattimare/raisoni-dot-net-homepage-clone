ANTARAGNI - GIVING VOICE TO THE INNER FIRE!

We at the Raisoni aspire to witness the same happiness in our students. The happiness which reciprocates in their day to day activities! The happiness which witnesses other shades of their personality! And, the Happiness in the form of giving a platform to showcase their talents

This has been the reason behind the fabrication and execution of ANTARAGNI.

WHAT IS ANTARAGNI?

The word Antaragni implies ‘inner fire’. It is the creativity or talent inborn, learnt or gradually developed. At the cultural festival, a podium is provided to the students to give voice to that inner fire!

  • It is a cultural festival.
  • It is organized by the Engineering colleges of RGI
  • A plethora of competitions are organized in here.

WHAT DO THE STUDENTS HAVE IN IT?
  • A platform – To showcase the talent the students have.
  • A comprehensive development- That awesome amalgamation of knowledge and creativityA comprehensive development- That awesome amalgamation of knowledge and creativity
  • Prizes- Accomplishments and achievements rewarded
  • Celebrity Night- Performance of the revered celebrities of the industry
  • An Exposure- A festival which is the meeting point of colleges around the region, from every nook and corner of the city gives the most amazing exposure to the talent of the students.

CELEBRATE EACH MOMENT WITH US
DANCE IT OUT
You may be impeccable in classical dancing, you might be talented in B-boying or you may have a talent for the Tango-come over and showcase your flair here at the cultural festival. And, if you are excellent get rewarded for it too!
SING LIKE A ROCK STAR
You always had that awesome singing gift with you. You polished it in the years. And today you sing like a star. At Antaragni we give you the podium to sing the lyrics like no one has done before.
SHOWCASE YOUR RAMP WALK
You think you have what it takes to be the next supermodel? Sway the ramp and prove it.
ACTING TALENT FOR THE STARS OF TOMORROW
you do not have blood flowing in your body, it is the acting talent which has occupied your mind, your body and soul. Nourish it further with a skit or a drama right here at our Cultural festival.
INSTRUMENTAL MAESTROS
Violin, Sitar, Tabla, Guitar, Santoor, Band, Flute- the name is enough to pick one right now and onset the devotional spree you have for the instruments. Antaragni gives you a platform to further showcase it.

These and many more talent shows to portray your talent on the best-in-the-class dais! Come live the Experience of a lifetime with Antargni! Portray. Discover. Rejoice.
