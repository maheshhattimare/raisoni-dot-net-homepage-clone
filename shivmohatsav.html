“NEVER BEND YOUR HEAD, ALWAYS HOLD IT HIGH” - CHHATRAPATI SHIVAJI

And, when one of the best cricketers quotes this you can not follow it.

The life of one of the greatest warrior and ruler of his time, Maharaja Chhatrapati Shivaji is an epitome of learning. The quotes he said had an impact back then, and are read and imbibed to date. His courageous acts have been mentioned in the history books, and they have left an imprint on the students, the historians and the learners alike.

Thus, we at the Raisoni, experience a feeling of proud mingled with happiness to celebrate the Shivaji Mahotsav with immense fervor, zeal and celebrations every year at our college campus.

What is Shivaji Mahotsav?
  • In order to pay tribute to valor, ethics, and principles of Chhatrapati Shivaji, Shivaji Mahotsav is celebrated in the college campus.
  • It marks a gala festival.

WHAT DOES IT INVOLVE?

  • The festivity is organized by the Student council known as the National Student Service Committee.
  • The most beautiful traditional dance programs make it to the fest.
  • The plays, the dramas and the skits signifying the role of the Great Maratha warrior- Chhatrapati Shivaji are performed here.
  • The martial art performances indicating the valor of the ruler are portrayed in the function.
  • The festivity is organized by the Student council known as the National Student Service Committee.
  • There is also a Celebrity attendance in the program.
  • The students wear traditional attire. The Girls wear Navarris and the Boys wear traditional Kurta!
  • All the attendees make sure to adorn their heads with saffron pethas.

WHAT IS IN IT FOR THE STUDENTS?

  • Execution and organization by the students belonging to the NSS Committee.
  • Participation by the students.
  • Understanding the stories woven around the valor and strength of the greatest ruler of his time through skits, dance, and drama.
