I think it’s fair to say that personal computers have become the most empowering tool we’ve ever created. They’re tools of communication, they’re tools of creativity, and they can be shaped by their user. - John Lubbock

And, we make sure to empower our students with this very tool in the form of computer laboratories. Our plethora of educational institutes under the RGI makes sure that the computer lab is nothing short of the par excellence amenities we have.

Attribute Which Bespeak Our Excellence

Equipped lab

Our computer labs are well equipped with the computers to enable students to practice and learn the basics of computer language and at the same time inculcate finer skills.

Furniture

Ergonomic furniture is positioned in the computer labs to aid in the comfort of students, teachers and faculty.

Internet speed

All our computers are well associated with high-speed internet facility. Thus, whether you, our students are trying to find about a new topic or downloading a particular academic paper, you can avail it with seamlessness.

Diligent staff

We do comprehend that you might not be completely aware of the working or functions of a computer. Or, you might be in need to gather more knowledge about it. Thus, we have the well versed and diligent staff right there at the computer labs. They will suffice you with a solution to all the queries associated with the machine.

Latest software

Indeed, the world of computers has something to witness almost every day. In order to keep pace with the most up-to-date in innovation, we make sure to update our computers with the latest software. This ensures the students learn better and be in pace with the current procedure.
