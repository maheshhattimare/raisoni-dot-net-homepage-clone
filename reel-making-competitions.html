To explore the creative side of our students, Raisoni Group brings you the most exciting Competition of this year, "The Reel Making Competition", with the theme "How I See My Campus". In this digital era, Reels have undoubtedly become a medium for the younger generation to exhibit their creativity and bring in the finest trends on social media.

So, we have decided to utilize this expressive platform and celebrate the creativity of our students together. All you have to do is submit a 30-60 seconds long reel and get the chance to be featured on our Instagram page.

Guidelines

  • The reel must be an original creation. You can only be inspired by existing trends online. Plagiarized content lead to disqualification.
  • Any offensive, name-calling, derogatory remarks, and/or regional slang towards any person or community should not be used.
  • The video should not exceed 60 Seconds.
  • The Competition is open to all Raisoni students from all campuses.
  • Individual, as well as group entries (not more than 3 people), are allowed.
  • Please use one medium of language for the reels. The submissions can be in either English or Hindi.
  • Each Participant will accept only one entry, and a second entry will lead to disqualification.
  • As per Reel format, the video should be made vertically and not horizontally.

Details of Submission:

The participants must send photographs to reelsforraisoni@raisoni.net on or before February 28th, 2022, 11:59 PM, along with the following details:

  • Name
  • Age
  • Profession (If any)
  • College Name
  • Residential Area
  • The caption of the photograph
  • camera details
  • Contact Number

Any Reel received after February 28th 2022, 11:59 PM, will not be considered for the Competition.


Prices

The top 3 entries will be winners who will get prize money worth INR 11,000, INR 5,000 and INR 3,000.


Terms & Condition:

  • Only Raisoni Campus Reels will be entertained.
  • Raisoni Group reserves the right to publish Reels in any online media without consulting the maker.
  • The decision of the Judging panel will be final.
  • We reserve the right to bar Entries for containing any infringing, threatening, misleading, false, abusive, harassing, defamatory, obscene, vulgar, inflammatory, scandalous, pornographic or profane content.
  • This Competition is open to all students of Raisoni. Each Participant agrees and acknowledges that Raisoni Group can receive their registration data by participating in the Competition. All personal information will be used as per privacy policy.
