Research is creating new knowledge - Neil Armstrong

Research and innovation go hand in hand. Some of the most amazing discoveries, inventions and innovations have been a part of the research and development pursued by the students during their educational endeavors. Research is the foundation stone for a rather promising tomorrow. It is the power which aids in transfiguring ideas into concepts, making sure to give birth to new theories and enhancing the strength called knowledge. More learning, in-depth information and novel scopes are the end products of research. Thus, we at the Raisoni ensure to inculcate this as a part of every institute under its umbrella. Our Research and Development cells are well equipped to promote research in a plethora of areas including, Engineering, Technology, Science and Administration. We make sure to give all that is required to shape the scientists, researchers, administrators and technocrats of tomorrow.

WHAT DO WE PERSUE?

Funding
The latest in Research and Development facilities requires funding. Thus, we at the RGI pursue proper funding in order to create new facilities and thus fabricating enormous opportunities.
Motivations
In order to make sure that the maximum number of staff and students participate in research activities, conferences, seminars and workshops are organized at the institute regularly. The staff and the students are continuously motivated to participate in them.
Tracking
A variety of competitions including, cricket, table tennis, tug of war, chess, carom, box cricket, pool. Rejoice and Indulge
Registration
The students are registered for the Ph.D programs for their contribution to the research and development.
Proposals
The faculty and the doctoral students are encouraged to prepare external research proposal. These are then sent to the various external funding agencies to avail funds for the projects
MOU
We have established partnership with a number of firms and industries in the form of MOUs. It makes sure to motivate research activities in our institution.
patents
Patents are an imperative aspect to be learnt by the students when working as a research associate. It is for this reason, our institute holds patent drafting awareness program. Comprehensive assistance is provided by our institute to aid the research students and faculty in the process of filing patents.
Seed money
At the RGI every step is taken to make sure that the students are motivated to do better. In the same context for innovative projects the seed money is provided by us.
For UG and PG
for our undergraduates and postgradate students' projects funding is also done by us.

WHAT ARE OUR RESOURCES?

Faculty Members
we have the most qualified, experienced and well read faculty.
Laboratories
Our laboratories are well equipped with research facilities to aid the students and research associated.
Study materials
The latest journals, both printed and online, multimedia and an amazing collection of books for inculcating research activities..
Research projects
We have research projects which are funded from external sources as well.
Research is the highest form of adoration - Pierre Teilhard de Chardi

And, it is this adoration we aim to implant in our students, making them learn better, bask in the depths of knowledge and enrich their educational journey.
